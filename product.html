HOW TO ORDER?

SHAPE

100% custom made = 100% custom shape. Every air freshener has its own design, so we don’t work with standard
templates. We can give you the total freedom for your shape, and to create your very unique and personal item.
Any shape can be cut as long as it fits in the 56 cm2 for our Standard production. Of course you can choose to have bigger sizes made, but that is not Standard production.

PACKAGING

Our air fresheners are individually packaged in a barrier coated
polypropylene film which prevents any loss of scent. This film has outstanding physical properties and has many
advantages over other plastic products preventing loss of scent and ensuring the best and lightest presentation. Each bag incorporates a health and advice message as standard. The air fresheners are supplied bulk packed in cartons.

HEADER CARDS

The standard product is supplied in the polypropylene film wrapper only.
We can also manufacture a header card to enhance the presentation, printed to your specification and attached to the freshener. These are quoted for separately on sight of artwork.

A header card is attached to the top of each air freshener bag. It has a hole for hanging on the wall or a counter display rack in your shop. The front of the header card has the name of your organization, club, etc. or can have any message you want. The back of the header card has instructions for the air freshener along with a bar code or can also be printed with whatever you want. The header cards are totally customizable and will be attached to each air freshener, Please ask our team how we can add an header card to your custom air fresheners.

Please do not hesitate to contact us to discuss your project.

CMYK PRINTING

All our airfresheners are cmyk full colour offset printed. This allows us to reproduce photo’s/ images in great detail and to achieve the desired end result. The cmyk printing process is the best way to achieve the quality of print that we expect on certain designs.

The growing demands of creating a promotional and marketing product that contains more information, greater detail and finer images forced us to fully change to cmyk full colour offset printing.
The new offset techniques now offers the possibility to successfully reproduce all designs in detailed full colour.

The 2 sides of the air fersheners can be printed in different colours/ different design, without any additional costs.

2 sides= 2 messages. As our airfresheners have a front- and a backside, you are totally free in designing your artwork. Front and back can be different without additional charges.

FRAGRANCES

Scent is the most enduring of our senses. Fragrance has the power to transform our emotions, heal our bodies and improve our memories.

More companies are discovering the power of fragrance to increase brand recognition, enhance sales and improve customer relations.

Air fresheners are products used to release fragrances in order to neutralize odours and to freshen the air/ the environment. These products are only applicable for use in indoor environments such as homes, offices, hotel rooms, cars and others. In recent years, this functional commodity has evolved to be used as a decorative product.

Now your business can be a hero to your customer or prospects by providing an air freshener that will keep their car smelling clean and promote your business on a daily business.These Air Fresheners can eliminate bad odors and even help restore that new car smell. Wide ranges of car air fresheners are available with different scents.

All our fragrances are made in the EU and they comply with both EU regulations as the IFRA guidelines (International Fragrance Association). IFRA is the official representative body of the fragrance industry worldwide. Its main purpose is to ensure the safety of fragrance materials through a dedicated science programme, helping both the consumer and the environment.

All fragrances can be supplied with up to date health and safety data sheets.
